Help/FAQ

General Questions

Q: How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, simply visit our website and use the search tool to find flights. Then, follow the prompts to select your flights and complete the booking process.

Q: Can I make changes to my reservation?

Yes, you can make changes to your reservation depending on the fare rules of your ticket. Some changes may incur fees, so it's best to check the terms and conditions or contact our customer service for assistance.

Payment Questions

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and various other payment methods depending on your location. You can view the available payment options during the booking process.

Q: Is it safe to enter my credit card information on your website?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption technology to ensure that your credit card information is secure when you make a payment on our website.
